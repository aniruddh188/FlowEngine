Our entire process is automated and managed for you. You only get involved when a new appointment appears in your calendar.

We launch ad campaigns (Facebook, Google) in a 10-15 mile radius of your service area, targeting homeowners actively looking for pressure washing.

2
Instant Lead Capture
Leads are sent to a high-converting landing page. The moment they submit info, our system engages them via Call, SMS & email in under 60 seconds.

3

Our automated system follows up, answers FAQs, and qualifies the lead, filtering out tire-kickers so you only talk to serious buyers.

4
Auto-Booked Appointment
Qualified leads are sent a link to your calendar. They book a time, and it appears on your schedule. No chasing, no phone tag. Just a booked appointment.
